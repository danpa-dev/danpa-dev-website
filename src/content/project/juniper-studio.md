Juniper Studio explores **who edits** versus **who reads**. The project content here focuses on workflow, not a product landing pitch.

## Intent

Designers needed to iterate on copy and imagery without touching build pipelines for every tweak. The system had to feel like “drop files and go,” with guardrails instead of gates.

## What I optimized for

Simple models, obvious previews, and diff-friendly storage. If an edit can’t be reviewed in plain text, the abstraction is too heavy.

## Reflection

The best CMS-shaped tool is often the smallest one that fits the team. I’d start even narrower next time and expand only when export pain shows up.
